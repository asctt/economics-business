Stage 5: Develop and present evidence-based conclusions and reasoned arguments in a statement of advice

Your work from Stage 3 on investment strategies and Stage 4 on risk management form the basis to plan a course of action that will accumulate future savings and manage finances for client. Include: * Proposed investment and risk management strategies * Recommendation: Amount to be allocated and/or action to be taken * Justification for advice Predicted consequences
