  • Weighting: 80%
  • Multimodal (digital presentation)
    • Presented in an electronic format (PowerPoint, webpage, OneNote, … see Methods)
    • Incorporate text and image at a minimum. Video or recorded voice could be used.

  • Completed in class time

Overview of task

The task is described below following the RAFT method: Role, Audience, Format, Topic (Santa, 1988, p.120).

Your objective is to conduct an inquiry and propose a course of action for client about strategies to manage finances and accumulate future savings in a a statement of advice presented electronically.

Role

You are a recent graduate [financial adviser] for Acacia Asset Management (AAM). You have been provided with a new client, 20-year-old Mr Aden Luck, who is anxious to understand how to manage his personal finances and build savings for the future.

Audience

Aden Luck is new to investing. You will need to provide clear explanations of your role and how you can assist him.

Format

You will create a multimodal presentation, such as a slide presentation () (see Conditions above and Methods) to explain investment options to Mr Luck and present your statement of advice.

You will need to consider your mode. This is a professional presentation; Images and text will need to fit together and follow a consistent theme. The design of this website is one model you could follow.

Topic

Convince Mr Luck to employ your services in following one of a range of appropriate strategies to achieve financial security in both the short and long terms through educating him about his options and risks.
